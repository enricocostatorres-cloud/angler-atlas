About Us

Built by Anglers,
for Anglers

Angler Atlas started as a passion project by a group of fishing enthusiasts who were tired of keeping catches in a notebook. We built the platform we always wanted — one that combines social sharing, data-driven insights, and community in one place.

Whether you're a weekend bass fisherman or a competitive tournament angler, Angler Atlas gives you the tools to fish smarter and share your story.

Features

Everything You Need on the Water

One platform for logging, discovering, competing, and connecting.

Interactive Maps

Discover fishing locations near you and see where anglers are catching fish in real-time with live GPS catch pins.

Catch Logging

Record species, weight, length, depth, lure, and conditions. Build a personal fishing logbook you'll return to for years.

Social Feed

Share your success, follow fellow anglers, like catches, and spark conversations about techniques and gear.

Leaderboards

Earn points for every catch, climb global rankings, unlock rank badges, and compete in community challenges.
